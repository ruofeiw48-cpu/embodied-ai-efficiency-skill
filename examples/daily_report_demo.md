# Daily Report Demo

This file shows a simplified example of the output that the Embodied AI Efficiency Skill may generate.

All data below is simulated and anonymized for portfolio demonstration purposes.

---

## 1. Today's To-do List

### High Priority

1. Follow up on tasks with delayed progress  
   - Check whether any collectors have not submitted enough valid data today.
   - Confirm whether the delay is caused by collection difficulty, device issue, or task unclear instructions.

2. Check quality review status  
   - Confirm whether yesterday's collected data has been reviewed.
   - Identify tasks with low pass rates or repeated rejection reasons.

3. Prepare delivery summary  
   - Confirm whether required files are ready for packaging.
   - Check if there are missing fields, incomplete records, or abnormal cases.

## 3. Risk Reminder

| Risk Type | Description | Suggested Action |
|---|---|---|
| Progress Risk | Some tasks may not reach expected daily output | Follow up with responsible collectors or suppliers |
| Quality Risk | Review pass rate may be lower than expected | Check rejection reasons and clarify collection standards |
| Data Missing Risk | Some fields may not be available for efficiency analysis | Confirm data source and field availability |
| Delivery Risk | Packaging or delivery status may be delayed | Prepare delivery checklist in advance |

## 5. How This Demo Supports the Project

This demo shows how the skill can help an intern quickly generate:

- A daily to-do list
- A progress summary
- A risk reminder
- A report draft for mentor communication

The goal is not to replace human decision-making, but to reduce repetitive summarization work and help interns focus on higher-value analysis.
